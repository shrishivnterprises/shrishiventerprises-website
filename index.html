About Us

Our Story

Shri Shiv Enterprises was established in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, with a single mission: to provide the highest quality stationery products at manufacturer-direct prices to schools, colleges, wholesalers, and retailers across India.

Over 15 years of manufacturing excellence, we have grown from a small workshop to a full-scale manufacturing facility producing millions of notebooks each year under our flagship brands — Vaishnavi, Siya, Harsh, Piyus, Anshika, Sainesh, Shivangi, and Sushant.

Our products are eco-certified, comply with all national quality standards, and are proudly Made in India under the Vocal for Local initiative.
